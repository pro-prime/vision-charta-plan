About

Read how Vision Charta Plan works and how to use it.

Adjust as life requires. Let it evolve with you.

Vision Charta Plan helps you manage your time by organizing both your tasks and commitments. It adjusts to your workflow, automatically schedules and prioritizes tasks, and keeps your day running smoothly. You can expect ongoing improvements as the app grows with user feedback.

How To Use
  1. Add tasks and blockers with the details you need, and your schedule will take shape naturally.
  2. You can also plan your schedule by heading to the "Ask AI" section and describing your tasks and blockers using simple, line-by-line instructions.
  3. Use Archives to review, restore, or permanently delete completed or removed items.
  4. Adjust settings to customize your work hours, breaks, plan horizon, and sound preferences.
  5. Use Visual, Queue, Backlog, and Blockers agenda views to manage your work and commitments.
How It Works
  1. Tasks are scheduled based on priority, deadlines, energy levels, and the time you have available.
  2. Blockers remove unavailable hours from your schedule, stay fixed in place, and influence task arrangement.
  3. Tasks can split into smaller chunks when needed and are always scheduled within your defined work hours.
  4. Plan Horizon determines how many days ahead the planner is allowed to schedule your work.
  5. Tasks automatically adjust and reposition themselves when priorities, deadlines, or available time change.
  6. Voice prompts and a live focus strip provide quick task input and real-time progress tracking.
